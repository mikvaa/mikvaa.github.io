I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of Turku. My current research focuses on housing preferences, how they change in response to life events, and how various characteristics influence these changes.

My other research topics focus on the economics of occupational choice: how temperament relates to self-employment, what kinds of economic preferences and temperament traits leaders have, and how these are reflected in occupational choices, and health economics: the economic impacts of physical activity, and the mobility of healthcare workers.
